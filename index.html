About Me

Hello there!

I'm Fazeela, someone who loves creating and celebrating things, come tangible or intangible, simple or sophisticated.
To be specific, I'm the most interested in how websites look and make you feel and how things peek in and hole up, slide in and away, and a myriad of other changes that adds to your experience when you surf the web.
Or what I gather as what front-end web development is all about. (Just why did I not say this already??)

Since I was a kid, I always loved to either reside or at least make frequent visits to those little and beautiful corners of web, that either taught me to create things, assisted me in learning, helped me find people with like-minds, and sometimes even throw in my judgemental/approving two cents on things that excited me and was important to me.
So, I guess my mostly positive experiences on the web, attention to detail and adaptivity to new things together as one inspired me to focus on web-design.

When I'm not typing away at my computer, you would find me still typing away at my computer, but designing and making my own outfits as well as custom-made dresses for my friends are things that fiercely demand my attention when I allow them to!
